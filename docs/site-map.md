## Home Page Section Order (Draft)

1. **Hero** — Brand tagline, airport-delivery value prop, CTA to vehicles or search
2. **Availability Search** (mock/staging) — Pickup location, dates, driver info prompt
3. **Benefit Strip** — Airport delivery, newer vehicles, real operator, transparent pricing
4. **Featured Vehicles** — 3-6 vehicle cards with real photos (or best available)
5. **How It Works** — 3-4 step simplified flow
6. **Airport Coverage** — MIA / FLL / PBI with links
7. **Reviews / Social Proof** — Turo reviews, Google reviews excerpt
8. **Trust Indicators** — Licensed, insured, South Florida local
9. **FAQ Preview** — Top 3-5 questions with links to full FAQ
10. **Final CTA** — Search vehicles or contact
11. **Footer** — Full navigation, contact, policies, language toggle
