# 305Fleet Rental Agreement Requirements — Draft

## Status: Draft — Not Active. Requires review by Ian and legal counsel.

## Airport-Related Provisions

1. **Guest responsibility for pickup parking** — Guest pays airport parking charge to exit garage at beginning of rental.
2. **Guest responsibility for post-trip retrieval parking** — Guest pays parking charge 305Fleet incurs when retrieving vehicle after rental.
3. **Premium Parking requests and charges** — When requested and approved, any parking cost differences disclosed to guest.
4. **Requirement to return to instructed location** — Guest must return vehicle to the airport facility and location instructed by 305Fleet.
5. **Requirement to provide location photos** — Guest must photograph the parked vehicle and surrounding location marker at return.
6. **Requirement to provide accurate garage, level, row, and space** — Guest must confirm in writing: garage, level, row/letter/zone, and space number.
7. **Requirement to leave garage-entry ticket** — Guest must secure the new garage-entry ticket with the key per the private procedure.
8. **Responsibility for excess parking caused by late return or incorrect parking** — Guest liable for excess parking fees.
9. **Responsibility for towing, citations, or unauthorized parking caused by guest conduct** — Guest liable for towing, citations, and unauthorized parking during rental period.
10. **Lockbox and key-return obligations** — Guest must follow the approved key-return and lockbox procedure.
11. **Charges for lost keys, lost lockbox, lost Faraday pouch, or missing ticket** — Guest responsible for replacement costs.
12. **Vehicle-return timing** — Vehicle must be returned by the agreed end date and time.
13. **Fuel or charging return requirements** — Vehicle must be returned with the same fuel level or battery state of charge as at pickup.
14. **Abandoned or improperly returned vehicle procedure** — Procedure for vehicles not returned to the instructed location or abandoned.
15. **Authority to charge the payment method for disclosed parking or operational costs** — 305Fleet may charge the guest's payment method for disclosed and documented parking or operational costs.

## Parking Fee Policy Summary

- Guest responsible for airport parking charge to exit garage at pickup
- Guest responsible for parking charge 305Fleet incurs during post-trip retrieval
- These obligations must be disclosed before booking
- Airport parking charges are separate from the vehicle delivery service
